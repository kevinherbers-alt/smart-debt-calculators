About Smart Debt Calculators

Smart Debt Calculators is a set of free, simple tools for one of the most common money questions: what's the fastest, cheapest way out of debt — and is paying it off early even the right move?

Every calculator here runs instantly in your browser. There are no accounts, no signups, and nothing you type is sent anywhere or stored. Enter your numbers, get your answer, share a link if you want to.

The tools:

  • Payoff vs. Invest — should extra money go toward debt or into the market?
  • Snowball vs. Avalanche — compare both payoff strategies for multiple debts.
  • Debt Payoff — your debt-free date and total interest for a single debt.
  • Extra Payments — the time and interest an extra monthly payment saves.

Smart Debt Calculators is part of the Swrl Tools family of free calculators. Questions or suggestions? Get in touch.
